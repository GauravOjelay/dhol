Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is my passion, and in today's age, it's more important than ever.

Hackers are becoming smarter and they're finding new ways around things, making it harder to keep people and information safe.

I believe cybersecurity isn't just about defending attackers, but staying one step ahead of them.

I want to be an ethical hacker to help organisations identify and fix security vulnerabilities before attackers can exploit them. Ethical hacking involves legally testing systems for weaknesses, using the same techniques as cybercriminals - but for defense, not harm.
