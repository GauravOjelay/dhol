Pricing

Many dhol players including myself do not publicly list their prices as they can vary widely, depending on factors like the location and duration.

Please message me via my social media for a quote.

Here's what you're paying for:

  • Skill and experience - I've invested years in mastering my craft. You're paying for my technical proficiency and my ability to play at a high standard for as long as you need.
  • Performance - I offer the energy and entertainment value that you'd expect a professional dhol player to provide to make your day special and memorable.
  • Off-stage work - In order to keep delivering a service of high standards, countless hours must go into practice and maintaining the dhol (for example, replacing worn skins, purchasing new sticks)

No matter the function, I can assure you that my performance will add great value to your event!
